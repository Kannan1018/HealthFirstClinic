Common questions

Things doctors ask us

Do I need to be in a specific city?
No — HealthFirst is pan-India. Whether you practice in a metro, tier-2 city, or smaller town, you can list with us. Patients searching in your city will find you.
Can I keep my private practice as well?
Absolutely. HealthFirst is an additional channel for you. Your private clinic, hospital affiliations, and walk-in patients all continue exactly as they are. We just add online bookings on top.
Who handles the payments?
Patient payments go through Razorpay directly to your linked bank account (minus our commission, if you're on the per-booking plan). For the subscription plan, you keep 100% of fees and we charge the flat monthly amount separately.
What if a patient doesn't show up?
Most patients who pay online show up — they have skin in the game. For pay-at-clinic bookings, you can require a partial advance to reduce no-shows. We're also building no-show tracking and reminders.
How long does verification take?
Usually 24–48 hours. We need a quick check of your medical registration number and credentials. Once verified, you're live the same day.
Can I leave anytime?
Yes. No lock-in contracts. Remove your profile anytime and we'll deactivate within 24 hours. Past bookings stay accessible for records.
