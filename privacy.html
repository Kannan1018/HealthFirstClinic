Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026

At HealthFirst, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect when you book an appointment through our platform, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using HealthFirst, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information we collect

When you book an appointment, we collect:

  • Personal details: your name, age, gender, and phone number
  • Booking details: the doctor you book with, the date and slot, your reason for the visit (optional)
  • Payment details: we do not store your card or UPI details — these are handled directly by our payment partner Razorpay, who is PCI DSS compliant
  • Account details (doctors only): email address used for sign-in

We do not collect government IDs, medical history, or any sensitive personal data without your explicit consent.

2. How we use your information

  • To confirm your appointment and share booking details with the doctor
  • To send you booking confirmations and reminders via WhatsApp or SMS
  • To allow doctors to maintain records of past visits
  • To improve our service (in aggregated, anonymous form only)

We will never sell your data to third parties, share it for advertising, or use it for purposes other than serving your appointment.

3. Who can see your data

  • The doctor you booked with — they can see your name, phone, age, gender, and reason for visit
  • HealthFirst administrators — for support and quality-assurance purposes only
  • Payment partner (Razorpay) — only when you choose to pay online

No other party can access your booking data. Doctors who delete their HealthFirst account lose access to their patient list immediately.

4. Reviews and ratings

If you leave a review after an appointment, your review and rating will be displayed publicly on the doctor's profile and on our home page. We mask your full name to protect your privacy (e.g., "Anita S." instead of "Anita Sharma"). You may request a review be removed by emailing us.

5. Data security

Your data is stored on Google's Firebase platform, which uses industry-standard encryption both in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Access is restricted by Firestore security rules — only you, the doctor you booked with, and HealthFirst admins can view your booking data.

6. Cookies and tracking

HealthFirst uses minimal browser storage to remember your bookings on your device (so the "My Appointments" page works) and your preferred language. We do not use third-party advertising cookies, analytics trackers, or behaviour-tracking scripts.

7. Your rights

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction — update incorrect information
  • Deletion — request that we delete your account and bookings (subject to legal record-keeping requirements for completed medical appointments)
  • Withdrawal of consent — opt out of receiving WhatsApp/SMS reminders

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@healthfirst.in.

8. Data retention

Completed appointment records are retained for 3 years in compliance with the Indian Medical Council regulations. Cancelled appointments are deleted after 90 days. Account data is deleted within 30 days of an account-deletion request.

9. Children's privacy

HealthFirst is not directed at children under 18. Parents and guardians may book appointments on behalf of minor children. We do not knowingly collect data directly from children.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated to you via email and noted on this page. Continued use of HealthFirst after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact us

Questions about your privacy or this policy? Email hello@healthfirst.in and we will respond within 7 working days.
