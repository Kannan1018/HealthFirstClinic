Welcome to HealthFirst. These terms govern your use of our doctor-booking platform. By using HealthFirst, you agree to these terms. Please read them carefully.

1. What HealthFirst is — and isn't

HealthFirst is a booking platform that connects patients with independent, verified medical professionals across India. We are not a medical provider. We do not employ doctors, provide medical advice, diagnose conditions, or prescribe medication ourselves. All medical services are provided by the independent doctor you book with — they are solely responsible for the care you receive.

2. Eligibility

  • You must be 18 years or older to create a booking on your own behalf
  • Parents or guardians may book on behalf of minor children
  • You must provide accurate, current information when booking
  • Each user may only register one account

3. Booking appointments

When you book through HealthFirst:

  • Booking confirmation is issued only after payment is verified (for online payments) or after the doctor's clinic accepts the booking (for pay-at-clinic options)
  • The fee shown at booking is the doctor's consultation fee. HealthFirst does not charge any booking fee to patients
  • You agree to arrive on time and follow the doctor's instructions

4. Cancellations and refunds

  • You may cancel any booking up to 2 hours before your scheduled slot for a full refund
  • Cancellations made within 2 hours of the appointment are non-refundable
  • If a doctor cancels your appointment, you will receive a full refund within 7 working days
  • Refunds are credited back to the original payment method via our payment partner Razorpay

5. No-shows

If you do not arrive for your booked appointment without cancelling, this counts as a no-show. No-show appointments are non-refundable. Repeated no-shows may result in temporary suspension from the platform.

6. Reviews

Only patients who have completed an appointment may leave a review. Reviews must be honest and based on your actual experience. We reserve the right to remove reviews that contain abusive language, personal attacks, false claims, or content unrelated to the appointment.

7. Payments

All online payments are processed by Razorpay, a PCI DSS compliant payment partner. HealthFirst never sees or stores your card or UPI details. For pay-at-clinic bookings, payment is collected directly by the doctor's clinic.

8. Doctor responsibilities

Doctors using HealthFirst represent and warrant that:

  • They hold a valid medical license to practise in India
  • They will maintain their schedule and honour confirmed bookings
  • They will provide care in accordance with professional medical standards
  • They will maintain patient confidentiality as required by Indian medical law
